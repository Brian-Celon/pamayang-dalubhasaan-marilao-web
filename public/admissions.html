Admissions & Enrollment

Everything you need to know to become part of Pambayang Dalubhasaan ng Marilao.

Step 1

Admission Procedure & Requirements

Entrance Examination (PDM-CAT)

a. Any senior high school graduate or senior high school candidate for graduation for the current school year is qualified to apply and take the PDM-College Admission Test (CAT).

b. Application for CAT may be filed personally by the applicant or his/her authorized representative (parents, guardian, relatives, teachers) at the Guidance and Counseling Office.

c. The examination will be administered by the Guidance Office from March to May. No one shall be allowed to take examination beyond this period as successful examinees shall be announced at least one month before the opening of classes.

Note: Applicants shall be allowed to take the CAT only once.

Requirements for Application

Interested applicants must submit the following requirements:

  • 2 pieces 2x2 ID picture
  • Filled-out Application for Entrance Examination Form
  • Certified true copy of grade 11 report card with 2nd grading period grade and 85% GPA for Computer studies applicants and 80% for other course programs
  • Proof of residence issued by barangay or school authority

On Examination Day

Applicants must present the application stub duly signed by the Admission Officer. No walk-in examinees will be allowed; all examinations are pre-scheduled.

Step 2

Enrollment / Registration Requirements

A student who passed the PDM-CAT shall be issued an Admission Slip through email. Upon verification of eligibility for admission, the student applicant must submit to the Registrar Office the following enrollment requirements based on their applicant type.

Incoming First Year

  1. Admission Slip issued by Guidance Office
  2. Filled-out application form for Admission
  3. Original copy of Form 138 (High School Report Card)
  4. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  5. PSA Certificate of Live Birth (1 original & 2 photocopies)
  6. Two (2) pieces 2x2 pictures (white background)
  7. Chest X-ray result (not later than 6 months)
  8. Marriage contract (for married female applicant)
  9. Two long brown envelope (ordinary) one long folder
  10. One long window letter envelope with postage stamp
  11. Photocopy of parent/guardian's Voter's ID/or any document to prove residency

Transfer Student

An applicant student seeking admission to PDM from another school must submit the following credentials:

  1. Filled-out Application Form for Admission
  2. Admission Slip
  3. Honorable Dismissal/Transfer Credential
  4. Official copy of grades or Transcript of Records for evaluation
  5. Two (2) pieces 2x2 picture (white background)
  6. Chest X-ray result (not later than 6 months)
  7. Marriage Contract (for married female applicant)
  8. Two long brown envelope (ordinary) and one long folder
  9. One long window letter envelope with postage stamp
  10. Photocopy of parent/guardian's Voter's ID/or any document to prove residency

Shifters

A student who wishes to shift from one course to another must submit the following to the Dean / Program Director:

  1. Summary of Grades
  2. Letter of Intent to transfer/shift noted by the Dean/Program Director
  3. Enrollment Clearance
