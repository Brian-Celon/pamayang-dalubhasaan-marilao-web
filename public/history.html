Milestones That Define Us

2010

Foundation

Pambayang Dalubhasaan ng Marilao was established by the Municipal Government of Marilao to provide subsidized, quality higher education to the residents of Marilao and neighboring municipalities.

2014

First Graduates

The institution celebrated its first batch of graduates, marking a significant milestone in its mission to mold productive and responsible citizens.

2018

Campus Expansion

A new academic building was inaugurated, expanding capacity for growing enrollment and adding modern classrooms and laboratories.

2021

CHED Recognition

PDM continued to strengthen its programs, upholding its commitment to academic excellence and receiving formal recognition from the Commission on Higher Education.
